About Our Society

The Tao Society Organization of Kathmandu is a community dedicated to the ancient wisdom of Taoism. Nestled in a city known for its rich spiritual heritage, we provide a space for individuals to study the Tao Te Ching, practice meditation, and cultivate a life in harmony with nature.

Whether you are a lifelong practitioner or simply curious about finding balance in a fast-paced world, our doors are open. We host weekly gatherings, philosophical discussions, and wellness practices right here in the valley.
